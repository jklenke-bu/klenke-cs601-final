My interest in computers and electronics began when I was in preschool. I started by taking my toys apart to try and understand how they worked. Later, I started playing on video game consoles and eventually computers. When I was old enough to get my driver's license, I started building and fixing computers for people as a way to make money.

After high school, I attended a trade school and traditional college before settling for online school. I earned a Bachelor's and Master's degree in IT related fields. Now I'm back again for a second Master's. Through that time, I've changed jobs a handful of times to go from Systems Administrator, Tech Lead, Ops Manager, Cybersecurity Analyst, and finally DevSecOps Engineer.
